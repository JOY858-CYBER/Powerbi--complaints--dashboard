This Power BI project provides a detailed analysis of consumer financial complaints, offering insights into complaint trends, product issues, company performance, and resolution effectiveness. The dashboard helps financial institutions, analysts, and compliance teams monitor complaint patterns and identify areas for service improvement.Project Overview

Consumer complaints are a powerful indicator of customer satisfaction and operational performance. This dashboard visualizes publicly available consumer financial complaints data and transforms it into actionable insights.The goal is to help stakeholders understand:

What types of financial products generate the most complaints````  `  `  

How companies respond to consumers

Whether resolutions are timely and effective

Geographic and temporal patterns in complaints

 Key Objectives

✔ Identify top products and issues generating the highest volumes of complaints
✔ Monitor complaint trends over time
✔ Analyze geographical distribution of complaints
✔ Measure resolution performance and timeliness
✔ Highlight company-level complaint statistics
✔ Provide insights to improve customer service and compliance processes

 Dashboard Features
1. Overview Page

Total number of complaints

Resolved vs unresolved complaints

Timely response %

Complaint trend over time (monthly or yearly)

Top financial products with the highest complaints

2. Product & Issue Analysis

Breakdown of complaints by product type

Top complaint issues (e.g., billing disputes, fraud, credit reporting issues)

Category-level performance indicators

Drill-down capability for detailed product/issue insights

3. Company Performance

Companies with the highest number of complaints

Response performance by company

Percentage of timely responses

Status distribution (closed, closed with explanation, in progress, untimely)



5. Time Series Analysis

Complaint volume trends by month/year

Seasonality patterns

Changes in issue categories over time



This project is for educational, analytical, and portfolio purposes.
Dataset used is publicly available and does not contain personal identifiable information (PII).
